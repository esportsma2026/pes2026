Download Effotbale from the App Store or Google Play. Create an account or log in, then choose your favorite team and start playing matches. The World Cup 2026 tournament is the main event!

Use the on-screen joystick to move your player. Pass, shoot, and tackle buttons are on the right side. Swipe for special moves like through balls and lob shots. Practice in training mode to master the controls.

The World Cup 2026 starts with 48 teams divided into 12 groups of 4. Each team plays every other team in their group. The top 2 teams from each group, plus the 8 best third-placed teams, advance to the Round of 32 knockout stage.

Score more goals than your opponent within the match time. Use strategy, good passing, and timely shooting. Defend solidly and counter-attack when you win the ball back.

In knockout rounds, if the score is tied after regular time, the match goes to extra time (2x15 min). If still tied, a penalty shootout decides the winner. There are no draws in knockout matches.

Play regularly, complete training challenges, and watch replays of your matches. Practice different formations and strategies. Join community tournaments to test your skills against top players.
